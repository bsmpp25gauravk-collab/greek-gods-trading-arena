The Five Greek Gods

Each Greek measures a different risk dimension of your options portfolio.

Δ
DELTA
God of Direction
Measures how much your option price moves when the stock moves ₹1. Range: 0 to +1 for calls, -1 to 0 for puts.
Call: 0 → +1  |  Put: -1 → 0
Γ
GAMMA
God of Acceleration
Measures how fast Delta changes. High gamma near ATM options means delta swings wildly — dangerous for short sellers.
Always positive for long options
Θ
THETA
God of Time
Time decay — how much value your option loses each day that passes. Option sellers love Theta. Buyers pay it daily.
Negative for long positions
ν
VEGA
God of Volatility
Sensitivity to implied volatility. A 1% rise in IV increases your option's value by Vega. Critical before earnings.
Always positive for long options
ρ
RHO
God of Rates
Sensitivity to interest rate changes. Positive for calls, negative for puts. Matters most for long-dated options (LEAPS).
Call: positive  |  Put: negative
